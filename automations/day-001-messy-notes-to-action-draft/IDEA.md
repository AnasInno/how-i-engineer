# Messy notes to action draft

## Persona

Solo founder, freelancer, operator, or job applicant who dumps rough notes into Telegram/Notes and later has to turn them into follow-up tasks.

## Pain

Messy notes often mix reminders, deadlines, people, and half-written actions. Cleaning them manually is boring and easy to delay.

## Current workflow

Read the note, identify action items, rewrite them as bullets, then decide what needs a deadline or follow-up.

## Input

`data/sample_input.txt`

## Output

`output/sample_output.md`

## One-day scope

A local deterministic CLI can parse a messy note into an action draft. It is intentionally not a full task manager or calendar integration.
